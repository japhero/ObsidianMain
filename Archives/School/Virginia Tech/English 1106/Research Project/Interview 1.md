# Questions

### Opening/Demographic Questions (Brief)

1. "What's your major?"
2. "How would you describe your academic performance in high school?" (let them elaborate beyond the number)

### The Transition Experience

3. **"Can you describe a specific moment when you realized college studying was different from high school?"**
    - _Why this works_: Gets concrete examples, not generalizations
4. **"Walk me through how you studied for a typical test in high school versus how you study for exams now."**
    - _Why this works_: Reveals specific behavioral changes (or lack thereof)




### The Autonomy Gap

5. **"In high school, how much of your studying was guided by teachers (review sheets, study guides, explicit instructions) versus figuring it out yourself?"**
    - _Why this works_: Directly addresses teacher-directed vs. self-directed
6. **"What's something you need to do to succeed in college that you never had to do in high school?"**
    - _Why this works_: Identifies the skill gap in their own words
7. **"Have you changed the way you study since starting college? If yes, what prompted that change? If no, why not?"**
    - _Why this works_: Gets at both adaptation AND resistance to change

### The Struggle

8. **"Have there been times when you studied hard but still didn't perform as well as you expected? What do you think went wrong?"**
    - _Why this works_: Explores the effort vs. results paradox from your survey
9. **"What's the hardest part of managing your own learning without as much teacher direction?"**
    - Thinking ahead, making sure to study a week before the exam not 2 days. Thinking longterm

### High School Preparation

10. **"Looking back, what do you wish high school had taught you about studying or learning?"**
11. **"Did high school teach you HOW to study, or did you just figure out what worked?"**
    - They tried but i really had to learn on my own and expanded that to here 

### Closing Questions

12. **"If you could give advice to your high school senior self about preparing for college academics, what would you say?"**
    - DO your work sooner and knock it out of the way and youll feel better
13. **"Is there anything else about the transition or studying in college that you think is important but we haven't talked about?"**
    - budgeting your dining dollars.
