
This movie is not something I would typically watch, a romcom, ughh, but after watching it maybe I shouldn't have come in so hot. I say this not because im infatuated with the love story or can relate to the way in which he attaches himself to summer but rather I think the movie displays a very real dynamic within relationships and more generally in life that i think is very relatable. Mainly that our brains shape and change the way we interpret the things other people do and say to mean whatever we want. The obvious example of this is the expectations vs reality scene which is a very relatable scene when you first watch it, i mean who hasn't had expectations shatter before. But more generally summer isnt really a good fit for Tom and he makes her out to be this obsession, all of her best traits he can describe about her are physical and later on we literally have the omniscient child literally say "you remember only the good parts". This movie just made me think about my own life alot and that's what makes me like it really.
