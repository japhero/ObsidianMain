**Topic Standing Waves**

- standing waves are created when 2 waves are created one sent and one reflected the interference in-between those waves is what creates the standing wave. 

Wave moving to the right $\rightarrow$
$$
D_{\rightarrow}= a\sin(kx-\omega t)
$$
Wave moving to the left $\leftarrow$ 
$$
D_{\leftarrow}=  a\sin(kx+\omega t)
$$

- Super position is where you add several waves together
