# Harmonic Frequencies 

- The amount of nodes and antinodes within the wave changes the length of the string relative to the different harmonics.
- Each harmonic is determined by the amount of antinodes more specifically its defined by the expression. 
$$
L=  m \frac{\lambda}{2} 
$$
> where $m$ is the number of antinodes

- Beat frequency 

$$
f_{\text{beat}} = |f_{1}-f_{2}|
$$

**Beat frequency**
$$
\frac{\omega_{1}-\omega_{2}}{2}=2\pi\left( \frac{f_{1}-f_{2}}{2} \right)
$$
