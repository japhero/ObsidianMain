
# Dump


| $n$ | $x$ | $y$ | $\frac{dy}{dx}$ | $dy$ | $y_{n+1}$ |
| --- | --- | --- | --------------- | ---- | --------- |
$dx=\text{constant}$
1. $n:$ iteration number
2. $x:$ $x$ value
3. $y:$ $y$ value
4. $\frac{dy}{dx}:$ slope of section
5. $dy:$  difference  (multiply given $dx$ by $\frac{dy}{dx}$)
