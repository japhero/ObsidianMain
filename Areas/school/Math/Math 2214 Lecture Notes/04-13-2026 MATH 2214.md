## Section 4.6 Complex Eigen values 

- Complex eigen values always occur in conjugate pairs.
- If $(\lambda,\mathbf{x})$ is an eigenpair, then $(\bar{\lambda}, \bar{\mathbf{x}})$ is another eigenpair.

**Theorem**
if $y(t)-u(t)+iv(t)$ is a complex valued solution of $y'=Ay$ where t is $(-\infty,\infty)$ then $u(t)$ and $v(t)$ is each a solution of this system.
