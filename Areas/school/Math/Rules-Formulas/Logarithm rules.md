
## Log of 1
$$
\log_{a}(1)=0
$$
---
## Log of the same number as the base
$$\log_{a}(a)=1$$
---
## Product rule
$$
\log_{a}(bc)=\ln (b)+\ln(c)
$$
---
## Quotient rule
$$\ln_{a}\left( \frac{b}{c} \right)=\ln (b)-\ln(c)$$
---
## Power Rule
$$
\log_{a}(b^c)=c\log_{a}(b)
$$
---
## Change of base Rule
$$\log_{a}(b)=\frac{\log(b)}{\log (a)}$$
