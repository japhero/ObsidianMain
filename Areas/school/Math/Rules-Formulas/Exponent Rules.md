## Multiplying and Dividing exponents 

$$
ax^m \cdot bx^n = ab\cdot x^{m+n} 
$$
> Multiplication

$$\frac{x^a}{x^b} = x^{a-b}$$
> Dividing 


---

## Adding and subtracting exponents

$$
\begin{aligned}
ax^n\pm bx^n &= (a\pm b )x^n 
\end{aligned}
$$

---
## Raising exponents to exponents and change of base
$$
\left(ax^m\right)^n = a^n \cdot x^{mn}
$$
> Distribution
$$(a^b)^n =a^{bn}$$
> Same bases

## Negative exponents 
$$
x^{-n} =\dfrac{1}{x^{n}}
$$
