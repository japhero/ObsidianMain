### Op AMps

- The two opp amp rules 
	- No current flows through the input terminals 
	- The two input voltages are equal (when there is negative feedback)
		- This only applies to opp amps and not to comparators

### Summing amplifier 
- Changes the gain equation 

**Non inverting**
$$
V_{out}=\frac{R_{f}}{R_{in}}(V_{1}+V_{2}\dots +V_{n})
$$
**Inverting**
$$
V_{out}=\frac{R_{f}}{R_{in}} = 1+\frac{R_{f}}{R_{in}} \cdot \frac{(V_{1}+V_{2} \dots + V_{n})}{n}
$$
