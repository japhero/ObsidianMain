## Decoupling Caps
- We usually use 2 decoupling caps one to get rid of high frequency noise and one to remove low frequency noise.

### Transfer functions with series filters
- When wave several filters we multiply the transfer functions to get the resultant transfer function 

$$
H_{f} = H_{1} \cdot H_{2}
