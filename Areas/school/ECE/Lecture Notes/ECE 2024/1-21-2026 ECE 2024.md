### Transformers 
- When two or more coils of wire are close to each other their magnetic fields will link with each other, inducing a voltage in the coils. As a result of this, the combined magnetic fields effectively create a new (third) one. This is called mutual inductance: when the magnetic field from one coil induces a voltage in another coil.

- voltage induced can be expressed as a fraction of voltages and $n$ number of turns per coil
$$
\frac{V_{1}}{V_{2}}=p\frac{N_{1}}{N_{2}}
$$
